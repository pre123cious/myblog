FRIENDSHIP

What is friendship?

Friendship is a state of being emotionally attached to another person resulting from the feeling of affection. It is also a warm and intimate relationship with someone you like and trust. It often does not involve any sexual interactions. Friendship is very important to adolescents because it helps to ease their transition from childhood to adulthood. It provides opportunity for acquiring experience which would aid the process of developing a self identity and promotes the ability to socialize.

TYPES OF FRIENDSHIP

There are different types of friends. While some are close friends, others are not so close, Yet others are mere acquintances, i.e. people you know but you do not consider as friends.
