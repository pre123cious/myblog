ADAPTABILITY

What is Adaptability?

Adaptability can be defined as the ability to change something or oneself to fit to occuring changes. It can also be described as the ability to cope with unexpected disturbances in the environment. In other words, adaptability is the ability of a system to adapt itself efficiently and fast to changed circumstances. An adaptive system is an open system that is able to fit its behaviour according to the changes in it's environment.

IMPORTANCE OF ADAPTABILITY

1. Your values will increase at your work place or school

2. Adaptability is a skill every leader must have

3. Adaptability create more happiness and over all life satisfaction.

4. Makes it possible for one to smoothly go through career changes

5. Whenever life knocks you down, bouncing back will become easy.
