MORALS

What is morals?

Morals can be defined as the act of being connected with principles of right and wrong behaviour. Morals can also be defined as the act of being based on your own sense of what is right and fair, not on legal rights or duties. It also means following the standards of behaviour considered acceptable and right by most people. In layman understanding, it means being able to understand the difference between right and wrong.
